Overview

Purpose

The Fitness Tracker helps users track workouts, nutrition, and progress toward their fitness goals. It provides tools to log daily exercises and meals, visualize progress through charts, and receive tailored workout and meal suggestions.

Audience

The target audience includes fitness enthusiasts, individuals beginning their fitness journey, and anyone interested in maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

Dynamic elements

The site will include:

  • Forms for logging daily exercises and meals.
  • Interactive charts visualizing progress over time (e.g., calories burned, meals logged).
  • Personalized workout and meal recommendations based on user input and goals.
